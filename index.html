About Me

Vytas Rudzinskas is a recent explorer of the world of web development and the broad world of programming. He is developing his skills in web development and transitioning those skills to other types of software development. He has been dabbling with HTML, CSS, C++, C#, and GameMaker Language for a number of years and finally got serious about learning to develop in 2017.

His other interests include rugby, tennis, baseball, video games, films, and anything that tells a story. He received a BA in Economics from UC Davis in 2010 with a minor in Chinese. Prior to web development, his primary career was in front of house hospitality including management.
